Chicken recipies

Description

Tender, smoky, and flavorful chicken—simple, healthy, and delicious. Great for any meal!

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts or thighs
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and herbs

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Chicken:
    • Clean and pat dry the chicken.
    • Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and herbs.
  2. Marinate (Optional):
    • Add olive oil and let the chicken marinate for 30 minutes to 2 hours.
  3. Grill the Chicken:
    • Preheat the grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
    • Grill the chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side (or until internal temperature reaches 165°F/74°C).
  4. Rest and Serve:
    • Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before slicing or serving.
