Pizza Recipe

Description:

Crispy crust, gooey cheese, and your favorite toppings—pizza is always a crowd-pleaser

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, and oregano

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  2. Add Sauce and Toppings:
    • Spread pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese.
    • Add your favorite toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers).
  3. Bake the Pizza:
    • Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
    • Transfer the pizza to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
    • Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  4. Finish and Serve:
    • Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano or red pepper flakes.
    • Slice and serve hot.
