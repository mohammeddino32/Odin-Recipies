Burger

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Ground beef or patty of choice
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo
  • Salt, pepper, and seasoning

Steps

  1. Prepare the Patty:
    • Season ground beef with salt, pepper, and your favorite spices.
    • Shape into patties and refrigerate for 15 minutes.
  2. Cook the Patty:
    • Heat a skillet or grill over medium-high heat.
    • Cook the patty for 3-4 minutes on each side (or until desired doneness).
    • Add a cheese slice on top during the last minute to melt.
  3. Toast the Buns:
    • Slice the burger buns in half and lightly toast them on the skillet or in a toaster.
  4. Assemble the Burger:
    • Spread mayo, ketchup, or mustard on the bottom bun.
    • Add lettuce, tomato slices, onion, and pickles.
    • Place the cooked patty on top.
    • Cover with the top bun and serve.
